## Updating your profile picture in Microsoft

Click here to begin updating your profile picture:

<LinkButton target="_blank" href="https://myaccount.microsoft.com/">Open the Microsoft Account Dashboard</LinkButton>

### Log in to your Microsoft Account

- Click the link above to open your [Microsoft Account Dashboard](https://myaccount.microsoft.com/). You will be prompted to log in to your LFBI.org Microsoft Account.
- Enter your Username in the *Email, phone, or Skype* field and click **Next**.
- Enter your Password and click **Sign in**.
- A prompt may appear asking if you'd like to *Stay signed in*? If you are using a personal computer, check the box next to *Don't show this again* and click **Yes** to finish the login process.

### Update your Profile picture

- On the card containing your name and email address, click the *pencil* icon next to the circle with your initials.
(This is a placeholder until you upload your own profile picture.)
- Click **Add a photo** and upload a profile picture.
- Click **Save** to complete the process.

:::note

It may take at least a day for your new profile picture to be shown on Microsoft Office and MyLFBI.

:::

## Updating your MyLFBI profile

Click here to begin editing your Moodle Profile: 

<LinkButton target="_blank" href="https://mylfbi.org/user/edit.php">Open the MyLFBI Profile Page</LinkButton>

### Log in to MyLFBI

Since you already signed in to your LFBI Microsoft Account, all you need to do is click the **365 Connect** button on the login page.

### Update your MyLFBI profile

- Under the *General* section, in the *Description* field, please write a short one-paragraph introduction. 
You could include your testimony, your local church, your ministry service, your goals at LFBI, etc.
- Under the *Optional* section, you can provide your current *Mobile phone* and *Address*.
- Under the *Other fields* section, please include your *Church Home*.
- Don't forget to click **Update profile** to save your changes!
